Other Cool Projects

RiemannWeb

RiemannWeb was a group math project where we could make any "product" to demonstrate our understanding. We chose Riemann Sums as our topic, and made a web app that can calculate the area of a drawn figure on a photo using Riemann sums. I was responsible for UI/UX and backend, as well as devops.


SPADE-GUI

On ElectronJS for Linux, SPADE-GUI is a GUI wrapper for NVIDIA's SPADE neural network that generates photorealistic images from semantic labels. I wanted a quick and easy way to interact with SPADE while other websites were down, so I made this.


WisdomDuck

This was our final project for Los Altos Hacks IV, after some of our other fabulous ideas didn't really work out in time. It dispenses wisdom and it's a duck. You cannot ask for more.
